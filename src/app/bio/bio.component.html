Bio

My name is Will Tian. I'm a software engineer based in San Francisco with two years of experience working as a fullstack developer.


A bit of background about myself. I initial trained in data analytics, having graduated with a degree in economics from UC Berkeley and worked as an analyst on stock portfolio performance at the campus California Investment Association. During my time there, I realized that alot of the hardest problems that I was facing stemmed not from data analysis, but rather from presenting such data in an intelligible and interesting way to our clients. I wanted to solve this problem and ventured into programming, via data visualization; from there, I became hooked, and have since worked on software development in the startup scene.
